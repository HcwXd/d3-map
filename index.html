When Will The Waste Kill You?

People dump a massive 2 billion tons of garbage every year. However, it’s hard for us to realize how severe this problem is.
We try to help people understand the problem by making this game. The game visualize how long will the garbage we dump can eventually cover the whole area of your country with certain given height.
You can adjust the four parameter on the left side to simulate different outcome. And also, you can switch between two differnet mode to view our planet by a simple click on the p button.

Waste Change per Person (kg/year)

How much waste do people increase or decrease annually.
Waste Growth Rate (%/year)
ⓘ
How fast the waste accumulation grow annually
Country Death Height (cm)
ⓘ
The height that defines a country's death.
In default example, a country is consider to be "dead" when the whole area of country is covered by waste with 100cm height.
Weight to Volume Ratio (kg/m³)
ⓘ
The conversion rate between waste weight and volume.
In other words, it defines the density of waste.
